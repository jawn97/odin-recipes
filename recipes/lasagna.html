Lasagna

Description

A delicious creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese combine to make this tasty lasagna.

Ingredients

  • creamy ricotta mixture
  • savory meat sauce
  • mozarella cheese
  • lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Spoon meat sauce on bottom of lightly greased casserole dish
  2. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles (or use no-boil or fresh noodles)
  3. Spread 1/3 of ricotta cheese mixture on top
  4. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce
  5. Repeat the same layers two more times
