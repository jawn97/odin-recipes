Homemade Pizza

Description

An easy to make homemade pizza recipe coming right up! Pizza fans rejoice!

Simple ingredients such as mozzarella cheese and dinner rolls help deliver a scrumptious pizza from your oven to your table in about 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 10 Rhodes White Dinner Rolls, thawed to room temperature
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups grated mozzarella cheese

    • Suggested Toppings

    • tomato
    • cooked sausage
    • pepperoni
    • onion
    • mushrooms
    • olives
    • green pepper

Steps

  1. Spray counter lightly with non-stick cooking spray
  2. Combine rolls together and roll into a 13-inch circle and place on a 12-inch spray pizza pan
  3. Cover with sprayed plastic wrap and let rise for 30 minutes
  4. Remove plastic wrap and spread sauce over crust
  5. Add desired toppings and sprinkle with cheese
  6. Bake at 425 degrees Fahrenheit 15 minutes minutes or until crust is golden
