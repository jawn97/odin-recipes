Popeyes Chicken Sandwich

Description

Made with toasted Brioche buns, crispy fried chicken breast, barrel pickles, and mayonnaise.

Ingredients

    Chicken Marinade

    • 2 1/2 lbs chicken breasts (3 chicken breasts)
    • 1 tsp seasoned salt (Lawrys)
    • 1 tsp baking soda
    • 2 Tbsp white distilled vinegar
    • 2 cups of water

    Wet Batter

    • 4 eggs
    • 1/2 cup flour
    • 1/2 tsp basking soda
    • 1/4 cup buttermilk
    • 1 tsp Turmeric

    Breading

    • 2 1/2 cups flour
    • 1/2 cup cornstarch
    • 1/2 tsp baking soda
    • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
    • 1 Tbsp seasoned salt
    • 3 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp adobo seasoning
    • 2 tsp white pepper
    • 1 tsp black pepper
    • 1 tsp paprika
    • 1 tsp garlic powder and onion powder
    • 2 Tbsp sugar

    Chicken Sandwich

    • 12 brioche buns (toasted)
    • 12 oz hamburger dill pickles (sliced)
    • 4 cups canola oil for deep frying
    • 1/2 cup mayo (divided)

Steps

  1. Slice each chicken breast in half crosswise. Cut each piece in half to make 4 fillets from each breast. (You should have 12 pieces all together).

    Pound the pieces with a meat mallet to 1/2" thickness if needed.

  2. Marinate the chicken in the chicken marinade for 20 minutes

  3. Meanwhile, make the wet batter and set it aside.

    4. In another bowl combine the breading ingredients and set them aside.

    Start pre-heating the oil over medium heat in a deep frying pan to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

  4. Drain the chicken from the marinade.

    Dipe each piece into the egg batter then into the breading.

    Coat the chicken well and shake off any excess flour

  5. Fry 3-4 pieces of chicken at a time (about 5 minutes per batch). Drain on paper towels

  6. To Make Sandwiches: spread 1 tbsp of mayo on each bun.

    Place 2 pickles on the bottom bun and top with fried chicken. Enjoy!
